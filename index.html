What We Do

We essentially sit around as friends and talk about our favourite teams on a weekly basis. We're not pundits, we don't pretend to be. We're just your regular fans who like discussing football with one another. But most importantly, we love laughing at each other's misery when there's reason to laugh and make fun of our friend's teams.
